# S4. Bootstrap diagnostic plots

*Part of the Supplementary Information for the manuscript “A mechanistic model of neutrophil-driven disseminated intravascular coagulation…” Main-text cross-reference: see Methods §2 and Results §3.*

Per-iteration cost distribution, parameter CI shrinkage with N, and basin classification of the factor XIII channel. The per-group-std normalization sensitivity (§3.2): under per-group std the fraction of iterations with negative Group II XIII R² rises to ~83% (vs 41% range-based), confirming that the XIII under-determination is robust to the normalization choice rather than an artifact of it.
